About

  • 2020-2022

    Our Humble Beginnings

    Operation falcon aims to provide offensive and defensive solution to organization cyber infrastructure. We aims to mitigate the risk as much as possible. At Operation Falcon Security (OFS), we aim to provide cyber solution to the company cyber problems by providing security assessment testing (penetration testing), asset discovery, attack surface management and offensive solutions. Operation Falcon seeks to provide offensive recon by monitoring and studying the behaviour of digital assets around the internet. In the initial stage operation falcon was just an initative which further turned out to be an Cyber security organization

  • January 2020

    Operation falcon is Born

    Operation falcon was born with a motive to provide cyber security and penetration testing services to clients.

  • January - December 2021

    Transition to Full Service

    As we all know that not every organization has the budget to keep good cyber security resources at their disposal. Therefore, they keep using the open-source community to have a good security posture. This led to rise of operation falcon cyber security solutions which are being used in organizations to reduce the risk factor as much as possible.
